About Me

Tim Alex is the lead programmer for all survey and reporting needs at Ironwood Insights. Tim is a recognized expert in the Survox data collection and reporting software. He has worked extensively with many Fortune 500 companies, executing research in industries ranging from automotive to healthcare. He has programmed surveys in more than 28 languages, which were fielded around the globe. Before coming to Ironwood Insights Tim was the Director of Online Programming at Thoroughbred Research Group for eight years where he created all automated reporting procedures, standards for internet and telephone interviewing, and quality control procedures for testing and data collection.

Away from work Tim enjoys spending time with family, traveling in his R.V. and fly-fishing with his sons.
